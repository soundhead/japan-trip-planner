Overview

Trip shape

This planner starts with a classic first-time Japan route: arrive in Tokyo, travel by rail through the Fuji or Hakone area, continue to Kyoto and Osaka, then fly home from either Kansai or Tokyo. It leaves room for day trips and slower rest days.

Once flights and dates are chosen, update the trip data in app.js. The destination cards, itinerary, and map are all driven from that file.
